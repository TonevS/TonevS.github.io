My programming experience

First exposure

My first programming experience was making projects with Python. I got exposed to it by a book "Automate the boring stuff with Python". By reading and doing the tasks I managed to finish one fourth of the book, until I reached a plateau and didn't know what to do or change.

Further advancement

I stopped bothering with it and asked my teacher about programming languages. I chose them and in 11th grade I started learning OOP in C#. Also in 12th grade SQL queries and C# LINQ queries.
